Recitation (কন্ঠনীড়)

As a presenter at Yuvavani, All India Radio, Kolkata, I bring the art of recitation to life through engaging in dynamic presentations. Join me for a journey of voice modulation, rhythm, and emotional expression as we explore various literary forms—from poetry to dramatic readings.

What You Will Learn:

  1. Voice Modulation: Learn how to control your voice to enhance expression and emotion.
  2. Rhythm and Pace: Master the art of timing, pacing, and rhythm to engage your audience.
  3. Emotional Connection: Discover how to connect with your audience through powerful recitation techniques.
  4. Creative Expression: Express your creativity through dramatic readings of poetry, stories, and more.

Recitation will help your child with better speech clarity.
